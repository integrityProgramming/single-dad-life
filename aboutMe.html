About Me



Hi, I'm Geoff an aspiring web developer and the father of 3 beautiful children; one of which passed in October 2009, a son who was born in 2013 and is autistic, and a beautiful daaughter who was born in 2015; and as of the title of this blog says I am a newly single father. Keep in mind I am not a single father because of a custody battle or anything like that, but I am a single father because their mother passed away.

A little bit about on the south side of Chicago. I have an associates degree in cyber security, a Bachelors degree in computer science, and a Masters degree in computer science. I have a passion for all things that lead to knowledge, which I try to instill on my children every chance that I get.

I have decided to create this blog for few reasons first I think that it will be theraputic for me to talk about the trials and tribulations of raising mixed children. Oh, did I forget to mention that? And, I also feel that it may be a good guide for someone else who is going through the same set of circumstances

Let me also state that this is a predicamint that I never thought that I would be in, therefore I am working through learning as I go, with the social services and such. I also am trying to keep in the forefront of my mind that my children will need some form of therapy as they are going through their individual coping strategies, and with that being said I am more then likely create 3 different posts to introduce my family one for Shanda (the mother of my children), one for Kelan (my son), and last but not least one for Kassidy (the baby of the family). Another thing that is worth mentioning is that I am also the caregiver for my mother who suffers from frontal lobe dimmentia, which as you can probably imagine adds a completely new level of difficulty to the equation.
